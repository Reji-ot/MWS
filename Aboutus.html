Our Concept

My wellness services, transpired from a thoughtful conversation, amongst friends. It helped us discern how a profusion of people are misled with the idea of wellness and how underutilized were our skill set and knowledge in this gamut. This induced into us a purpose on how we should put our know-how to use, for the benefit of humans as one entity and thus ‘My Wellness services’ saw its day light. The purpose wasn’t to reinvent the wheel, but rather present the yogic principles in its truest form whilst also introducing a wider range of wellness services, that delivers comprehensive well being, when practiced in the right sense. As you browse through these pages, you will come across an array of program, each curated to ameliorate your overall wellness.

Our Experts

GURU DHEERAJ

A maven in Yogic practice and principles. Dheeraj ji was born in Rishikesh- India (Global Capital of Yoga) and thus his bond with Yoga was innate in many ways. This however, didn’t limit him from acquiring professional coaching in disparate health program. He completed Masters in Yoga (India, 2013), Certification in Romana Pilates (Mauritius, 2013), Diploma in Yogic Science (India, 2006) and 200 Hours of Yoga. He has participated in numerous seminars that highlighted the significance of Yoga, Psychotherapy and spirituality in modern aeon. He never shied away from edifying others with the cognizance he possessed. In the initial days, he joined as a Yoga instructor with a school in his neighbourhood but soon travelled across Asia and continued in his teaching journey. He worked in Jendya Yogic Centre, Zhengzhou- China (2007-2009), Shanti Maurice & Four Seasons, Mauritius & Chiang Mai (2009-2021) and in present day he is associated with ‘My Wellness services’. We are as much as pleased as you to have Dheerajji join and willing to guide our premium members, in performing yoga in its authentic form.

Dr RAJA

A stalwart in the space of alternate medicine, healthcare and fitness. He hails from a traditional Tamil family, where the mothers make use of various spices, herbs and medicinal leaves to cure ailments, eg: cold, fever, headache etc. Growing up, he learnt from his parents about the innumerable herbs and spices used in their household and their affects on our health. His early interaction with alternative medicine prompted him to look beyond modern medicine for cure. He completed Bachelor’s degree in Naturopathy & Yogic Science (BNYS) from T.N Medical College of Naturopathy and Yogic Science. He went on to hone his skills in several alternative therapies like Mud therapy, Hydro therapy, Fasting, Iridology, Diet therapy and Counselling. He also possesses a deep understanding and practices Asian therapies: Acupuncture & acupressure, Reflexology, Shiatsu, Chi nei tsang & Toksen. He is well versed with conducting various wellness program, diet counselling and nutrition. Over the years, he has been part of many health programs that focuses on alternative healing therapies.
